  • Originality: What is original about my game is not much since im sure its been done before but i added new mechanics from the base code like having points to gather and having them start from the corners.
  • Technical Merit: What is interesting about my game technology is that the enemies follow you from wherever you are and when the enemies get to close to one another they spawn new enemeies.
  • Prototype Postmortem: What was the easiest or hardest part of making it? What I learned from this prototype is a lot actually. I learned the === doesnt really work when working with sprite locations and how sprite movement can work. The easiest part of the prototype was making the points sprite. The hardest part was getting the sprites to respawn when they get too close to one another.

  • Player Experience Goals: The experience I want players to have when playing my game is to just have a good time.
  • Gameplay: The actions that you can do is use the mouse to move around the screen picking up the votes that will give you 10 points each. Your goal is to pick up as many votes as you can. The opposition in my game are the Trumps that follows you around the screen. Their goal is to grab all the votes from you. The way the game ends is when you get hit by a Trump.
  • Strategies: Some player strategies I expect that will be effective at playing my game is using the respawn time to grab points and make Trumps respawn to move more freely.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The world my game is set in is the real world during the 2020 election where Trump is running again and trying to steal as many votes as he can. What makes the game world and its occupants unique and interesting is that its all a meme but real at the same time. The backstory of the game is I started with a random follower game and my friend said to change GAME OVER to Thanks Obama. Then a political statement grew from that. Trump trying to steal all the votes from his competitors.
  • Target Audience: The demographic I'm trying to reach are the young voters that will understand this is a meme of a game while also realizing the sad truth that this is what he actually does.
  • Play Time: ~ 30 seconds a round.
